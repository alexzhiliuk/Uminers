{Roots and development}

Uminers, a leading international supplier of cryptocurrency mining equipment, was founded in 2017 as an offshoot of UASIA GROUP, which began operations back in 2010. The initial request to supply miners for the Dash cryptocurrency opened up a new horizon for the company and served as the beginning for Uminers.

2017

Foundation of the company

{First steps on the world stage}

A key moment was participation in the World Digital Mining Summit (WDMS) in 2018 in Georgia, where contacts were established with leading manufacturers of mining equipment, including Bitmain and Whatsminer. This event laid the foundation for the successful development of the company.
