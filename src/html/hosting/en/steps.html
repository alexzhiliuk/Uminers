Order

Clients select a hosting plan that fits their mining needs and place an order with UMINERS. This includes providing details about their mining equipment and setting up payment for the hosting services.

Delivery

Clients can either ship their mining equipment to our data center or our team can arrange to collect it for them. Upon receipt, UMINERS specialists will inspect the equipment and begin setting it up in the hosting facility.

Installation

Once the mining equipment is installed and configured, our team will start hosting the equipment for the client. This involves ensuring that the equipment is running efficiently, monitoring performance, and addressing any technical issues that may arise.
